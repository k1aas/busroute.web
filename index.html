BusRoute

Voice guidance for substitute school bus drivers

When a regular driver calls in sick, the substitute needs to know the route. BusRoute lets the regular driver record their route over a few runs, then hands off a complete voice-guided profile to whoever drives that day.

No looking at the phone. No paper maps. The app speaks — the driver drives.

Android Works offline No account required Voice only during driving

How it works

📍

Regular driver records the route

GPS stop detection runs automatically. After a few runs, the app averages stop positions and arrival times into a reliable profile.

📱

Profile shared via QR or file

The driver generates a QR code or JSON file and sends it to the branch office. Office files it and forwards it to the substitute on the morning it's needed.

🎙️

Substitute drives by voice

The app announces each stop approaching, expected student count, and any special notes — all by voice. The screen stays dark. The driver stays focused.

🔒

No internet required. No data collected.

Everything runs on-device. No accounts, no cloud, no student data ever leaves the phone. Route data expires automatically after 105 days.
