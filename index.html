BusRoute

An effective solution to a decades-old problem

When a regular driver calls in sick, the substitute needs to know the route. BusRoute lets the regular driver record their route over a few runs, then hands off a complete voice-guided profile to whoever drives that day.

No looking at the phone. No paper maps. The app speaks — the driver drives.

Android iOS Works offline No account required Voice guided Turn-by-turn navigation

How it works

📍

Regular driver records the route

GPS stop detection runs automatically and silently. After a few runs, the app averages stop positions and arrival times into a reliable profile — no interaction needed while driving.

📱

Profile shared via QR code or file

The driver generates a QR code or export file and sends it to the branch office or directly to the substitute. Turn-by-turn route guidance is embedded in the profile.

🎙️

Substitute drives by voice

The app announces each stop approaching, expected student count, special notes, and turn-by-turn directions — all by voice. Distance to the next turn is shown on screen. The driver stays focused on the road.

🔒

No internet required during the run

Everything runs on-device. No accounts, no cloud, no student data ever leaves the phone. Route data expires automatically after 105 days. Internet is required only once — when activating a license key.

⚖️

Safety and legal compliance

Requiring a substitute driver to navigate an unknown route without guidance creates legal risk for the employer. BusRoute addresses this directly — the driver is guided, not distracted. CIPO Registered, Reg. No. 1245935.
